DEVICE VERIFICATION

IMEI CHECKER

Enter a device IMEI or serial number to check for blacklist status, activation lock, and authenticity signals. Always check before you buy.

📞 To find IMEI: dial *#06# on the device, or check Settings → General → About (iPhone) or Settings → About (Samsung).

WHAT IS BLACKLISTED?

A blacklisted phone has been reported stolen, lost, or has unpaid bills. Carriers block it from their network. Never buy a blacklisted device — you can't resell it.

ACTIVATION LOCK WARNING

iCloud or Google account lock means the previous owner's account is still linked. The buyer can't set it up. Always ask for a lock-free device before purchasing.

IMEI VALIDITY CHECK

A real IMEI is 15 digits and passes the Luhn algorithm checksum. A fake or cloned device will often fail this check — a red flag for counterfeit hardware.

FREE LOOKUP SITES

For a full carrier blacklist check use: imei.info, swappa.com/esn, or your carrier's IMEI checker. Some checks cost $1–5.

💡 RESELLER PRO TIPS
• Always negotiate — most sellers expect it. Offer 10–15% below your max buy price first.
• Carrier locked phones sell for 15–20% less. Only buy if price reflects that.
• "For parts" phones are goldmines if you know what you're doing — screens, batteries, housings.
• New sealed boxes command a premium — verify seal authenticity before paying top dollar.
• Factor in your time: if it takes 2 weeks to sell, that cuts your effective margin.
• Check eBay "Sold listings" to verify current real-world resale value before buying.
